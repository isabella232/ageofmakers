What Will You and the Students Learn?

The Music for Change Journey in the Age of Makers game has 5 learning areas placed on a virtual map:

  • Changemaker Camp: History of music & social change
  • Music City: Music theory & production
  • Lyrics Valley: Write your own lyrics
  • Beats Forest: Kick, snare and all that jazz.
  • Lake of Effect: Use classic sound effects.
  • Mixing Mount: Mixing & exporting tracks #finalTouches
